Social Media Comparison

Facebook
Facebook is used for sharing photos and videos with others. You are also able to message and interact with people and communities.
Twitter
Twitter is used to share short text and photo based ideas and thoughts with others. Twitter is known for having limits on the amount of text per post.
Youtube
Youtube is a social media platform used for sharing videos with others. users can share and comment on videos.
